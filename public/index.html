I am Brad Christensen

Designer, developer and geek

A little about me

I'm a software developer at Enlighten Designs in Hamilton, New Zealand, primarily involved with the front-end development of ASP.NET web applications. I'm currently contracted to Livestock Improvement Corporation as an agile developer working on MINDA for the Web.

I previously worked as a front-end developer for Canterbury-based Kite Media, and I was a graphic designer for H&A Print in Whanganui.

In 2014 I wrote my honours thesis on IPv6 networking for embedded devices running the Contiki operating system. I used the 6LoWPAN IPv6 abstraction layer to enable communication between devices on the Internet of Things with the Constrained Application Protocol (CoAP).

Things I like to do

I craft pixel-perfect websites using a variety of modern technologies, and I like to be involved in every part of bringing them to life. That sometimes means I am a wannabe Debian sysadmin and other times I might fancy myself a bit of an illustrator. Sometimes I even talk to people.

Most of the time I like to occupy myself with code and have been particularly enjoying writing Javascript lately, both for the browser and the server.
