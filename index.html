I am Brad Christensen

Designer, developer and geek

A little about me

I'm a fourth year computer science honours student at the University of Waikato in Hamilton, New Zealand. I moonlight as a front-end developer for Kite Media, a digital creative company based in Canterbury, and occasionally I am a print designer (or web architect) at H&A Print in Wanganui.

Of course, I should really be spending all that time working on my honours project, which is in the area of Contiki-based IPv6 networking for wireless sensor networks and the Internet of Things.

Things I like to do

I craft pixel-perfect websites, design for printed and digital media and create my own software from time to time. I write clean, MVC-based sites and I like classic web technologies like jQuery, SASS, and the Apache/MySQL/PHP stack or Python's Pyramid/Pylons.

Like most WAND members, I enjoy a good curry from time to time. Come and join us if you want to have a chat over excellent food in great company.
