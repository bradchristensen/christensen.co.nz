A little about me

I'm a Computer Science honours student at the University of Waikato in Hamilton, New Zealand, originally from Wanganui, where I am still an occasional graphic designer at H&A Print. I am currently working for the WAND network research group on network visualisation. My main interests are in networking, web design and mobile software development.

And some of the things I like to do

I craft pixel-perfect websites, design for printed and digital media and roll my own software from time to time. This website will show you some of the more interesting things I've worked on in the past few years (well, it's kind of like work).
