I am Brad Christensen

Designer, developer and geek

A little about me

I'm a software developer at Enlighten Designs in Hamilton, New Zealand, primarily involved with the front-end development of .NET web applications. I'm currently contracted to Livestock Improvement Corporation as an agile developer for the MINDA Live programme.

I previously worked as a front-end developer for Canterbury-based Kite Media, and I was a graphic designer for H&A Print in Whanganui. I also wrote an honours thesis on IPv6 networking for the Internet of Things.

Things I like to do

I craft pixel-perfect websites using a variety of modern technologies, and I love to be involved in every aspect of bringing them to life.

I'm also currently trying to teach myself Russian. It is an uphill battle.
