I am Brad Christensen

A little about me

I'm a software developer at LIC in Hamilton, New Zealand, primarily involved with the front-end development of MINDA Live and other .NET web applications.

I've previously worked as a graphic designer for H&A Print in Whanganui. I also wrote an honours thesis on IPv6 networking for the Internet of Things, before it was cool.

Things I like to do

I craft pixel-perfect websites using a variety of modern technologies, and I love to be involved in every aspect of bringing them to life.

I'm also currently trying to teach myself Russian. It is an uphill battle.
